Returns & Exchange

Returns and Exchange requests will be catered in the following 2 Cases.

Case 1: Damaged, Broken, Leaked, Incorrect, or Missing Item

• Claims must be submitted within 24 hours of delivery.

• Provide clear images of product and packaging.

• Product must be unused and in original packaging.

• Refund or replacement will be processed after verification.

Case 2: Exchange or Change of Mind

• Product must be returned in original condition.

• Delivery charges may apply for exchanges.

• Exchange will be processed after quality check.

Case 3: Out-of-Stock Refunds

• If a product is out of stock, refund will be processed.

• Refund takes 5-7 working days.

Timelines

• Claims must be raised within 24 hours of delivery.

• Complete resolution may take 5-7 working days.
